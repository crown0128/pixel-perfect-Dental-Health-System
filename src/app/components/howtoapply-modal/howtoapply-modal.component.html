How to apply for a payment option

Waiting to see your Dentist?

While we'd love to tell you how much you'll need for your next dental treatment, the reality is you need to see your Dentist and have them present you with a proposed 'treatment plan' outlining the cost involved.

Once you've seen your Dentist, they'll send you an invitation for treatment via email, which will include personalised payment options to help with the affordability of your next treatment.

Chosen the payment option that best suits your needs, and you'll be guided through the convenient, automated application process (which only takes a few moments to complete) - you will get real-time feedback on your application and once approved you're all set to proceed!

Already visited your Dentist?

Great! If you haven't already received an invitation for treatment from your Dentist via email, simply get in touch with them and request one. They'll send you an email with details of your dental procedure, and the options apply for a payment plan, or personaly loan will be presented.
