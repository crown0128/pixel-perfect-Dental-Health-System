So if it is for FREE, how do we maintain a financially viable business?

Whilst we strive to help practices improve patient communication and marketing, the way we get paid is when patients finance their treatment. We know not everyone is interested or needs to finance treatment however, how do you know who does?

The only sure way to know is to present all treatment the same way with payment options to everyone, hence why we have built a communication platform that caters for patients electing to proceed several ways or just provide feedback.

When patients require payment options to afford their treatment, we are here to help with competitive payment plans and personal loans. You can only achieve affordable treatment in your patient minds by presenting digitally with offers for finance included.

With our business model, the only way we can have the opportunity to make money is if more of your patients accept treatment, thus aligning our mutual interests.

Hence our focus to provide the best possible platform for all your patient communication, marketing, compliance, and financing needs.

More treatment acceptance means a win for both you and Smile Right.
