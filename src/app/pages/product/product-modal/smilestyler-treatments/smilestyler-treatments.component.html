What we treat Get a smile that's designed to shine

SmileStyler® has been designed to not just straighten your teeth, but also to positively affect the aesthetic features of your face.

OVERBITE

Upper teeth protrude over the lower teeth by a proportion of between 30-50%.

CROWDING

The lack of space for all the teeth to fit normally within the jaws. The teeth may be twisted or displaced.

SPACING/DIASTEMA

A dental anomaly characterised by interdental spaces and lack of contact points between teeth.

ROTATIONS

A condition where one or more teeth are not aligned properly in relation to the adjacent and opposing teeth.

CROSSBITE

A malocclusion where a tooth (or teeth) are inset behind the inside of a corresponding tooth.

OPENBITE

Occurs when the front teeth fail to touch and there is no overlap between upper and lower incisors.

UNDERBITE

A condition in which the lower teeth and jaw protrude in front of the upper teeth.

DENTAL MIDLINES

This type of problem is caused when the bite does not fit and match appropriately.

BUCCAL CORRIDORS

Negative space present between the lateral aspects of the back teeth and the corner of the mouth during smile which appears as a dark space.

GUMMY SMILE

A ‘gummy smile’ is one where the gums show when you are smiling.

OVERJET

Refers to the horizontal distance between the upper front teeth and lower front teeth.

Results do vary between individual cases and ideal aligner cases are those that require moderate alignment or rotation. Talk to your dentist or orthodontist about whether SmileStyler is the right solution for your dental issues.
