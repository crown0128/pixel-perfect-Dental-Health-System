Sending Treatment Plans

Paper is a thing of the past, it’s time to go digital! We have made thing easy with just a few clicks you will be communicating digitally in no time.

What do I need to do for my patients?

Depending on how you run your practice, treatment planning and presentation can either be done directly by the practitioner or assigned to the reception/treatment coordinator. As the practice manager, you may not be directly engaging with patients. However, you need to ensure that patient data is uploaded and your settings configured for things to run smoothly.

An essential thing for getting started is defining your treatment templates. Templates help save time when sending treatment plans by reducing treatment planning to a few keystrokes and, in most cases, less than 30 seconds!. Use the examples provided by Smile Right, modify to suit your practice or create your own from scratch.

Getting Paid

Provided treatment? Now it’s time to get paid by putting in a settlement request.

Settlement Request

Once approved for finance, Smile Right is now responsible for settling the patient's bill so you can proceed with confidence.

Simply provide the treatment and request settlement as the work is performed and invoiced to the patient. There is no limit to the number of requests you can make.

done

New Settlement Request - In your blue menu bar go to settlements > new settlement request. You can also press the blue "piggybank" at the top of every page. Search your patient and select their treatment plan.

feed
done

Settlement Amount - Type the total amount completed for this treatment according to the invoice you provided. Does your patient have private health insurance and wants to make a claim? Process the claim via your HICAPS terminal then via the portal select the fund, and add the amount they have been rebated. This will update your settlement request, and we will apply the payment automatically to their payment plan balance. For more information about this, read the “how-to” article to the right.

feed
done

Authorise Settlement - Once happy with your request, confirm settlement with your patient. We will send an SMS with an authorisation code. You need this code to continue with the settlement request. When you have the code, your patient is good to go.

feed
done

Finalise Settlement - Upload a copy of your treatment invoice and submit for processing.

feed
done

Tracking Payments - View your request status by going to your blue menu bar and pressing settlements > settlements. You can see your "approved" patients and the maximum value you can claim. Click on the patient to see a detailed view of all claim and payment made.

Helpful Tools

At the top of every screen, you will find the following valuable tools.

zoom_in

Product Lookup: Used to search for all the supplier products available on the platform and will display any information relating to the product selected, including supporting documents, images and videos. Search by Item Code, brand or product. Can also be used for a variety of things, including research, staff training, patient presentation, invitation templates, treatment planning and more!

Financial Calculator: This is a helpful feature! If a patient is enquiring about payment options, costs or repayments, use this feature to show/tell your patient and answer any query they might have on Smile Right payment options. Input the treatment value, and the calculator will show all options available.

attach_money
notifications

Activity Bell: Every action or interaction either by a team member or patient that happens on the platform will give you a notification. It would help if you viewed these notifications regularly to stay up to date with what is happening. You can mark notifications as read, or "star" them if they are essential and return to them later. To view a full audit trail, click the communications tab in the blue bar and see activities and communication to and from the platform.
