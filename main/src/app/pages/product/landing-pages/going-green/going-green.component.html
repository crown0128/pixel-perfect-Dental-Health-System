Thinking about your next visit?

1 RECEIVE

Once you have seen your dentist, you will be sent your proposed treatment securely via email and SMS.

Click on the link provided, and it will take you to your own personalised digital treatment plan. View as many times as you like as this is available even after you have decided on the treatment.*

2 REVIEW

Depending on the treatment proposed, you will see the following to assist you in being better informed:

  • Proposed treatment
  • Before and After images
  • Important information
  • Fact sheets
  • Instructions for use
  • Videos

Information is presented in such a way as to inform and educate before you decide to proceed.

3 DECIDE

When you are ready, let your dentist know how you wish to proceed. Options include:

  • Apply for a payment plan
  • Pay cash or card on the day.
  • Hold off for now
  • Request changes
  • Decline treatment
4 REQUEST

Once you have decided to proceed, you can request an appointment online at a time that best suits you!

*Past treatments may be accessed via our patient portal.
